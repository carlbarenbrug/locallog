Hi. Welcome to Local Log.

This is your private space to capture thoughts as they happen.

Use it to journal your day, process emotions, track ideas, or record personal reflections.

Local Log is designed for three kinds of entries:

1. Text
- Write quickly, without overthinking.
- Capture raw thoughts, plans, and notes.

2. Audio
- Speak your thoughts when typing feels too slow.
- Useful for walks, voice memos, and stream-of-consciousness reflection.

3. Video
- Record face-to-camera entries to document mood, context, and story.
- Useful for personal logs, progress updates, and deeper reflection.

Everything is local-first.
Your entries live on your machine.

Simple workflow:

1. Open Local Log.
2. Start a new entry.
3. Choose text, audio, or video.
4. Capture what’s on your mind.
5. Review later and track patterns over time.

Tips:

- Don’t optimize for perfect writing.
- Aim for honesty and momentum.
- Short, frequent entries beat rare long entries.

You can use Local Log for:

- Daily journaling
- Work logs
- Decision tracking
- Emotional check-ins
- Idea capture
- Weekly reflection

Start now.
Write or record one small entry:

"What am I thinking about most today, and why?"
